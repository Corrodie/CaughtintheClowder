We've Moved!

Hey everyone! Woah! Look at this nifty new site! Why and how did that happen?

I have been studying and trying to pursue a secondary job, which required me to build a portfolio website - in doing so I found a way to host that page for free and thought...can I do that for the blog, too? When am I going to be charged for that anyway?

Once I realized that the deadline was approaching (my charge date for wordpress) I decided I'd better start coding, and so the port-over process began. It's been a lot of work, but it's saved me money and in the end I think it will be worth it. Eventually I'd like to get my custom domain back, but for now this will do.

I hope you all like the new digs! See you soon.

Until next time.

-Meg

About

Hi, I’m Meg. A cat mom of 7 and, well, overall cat lady. I’d been pretty lost in life until back in 2016 I decided to start volunteering in animal welfare and very quickly developed a passion for feline behavior. I started learning everything I could – and still am. I began working in the field and now head the behavior department at my local shelter. I don’t get to simply educate as much as my aspie brain would like to, so, I thought, why not write about it?! And here I am.
